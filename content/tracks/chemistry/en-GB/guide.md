# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## chemistry assignment tips

- **Reaction & mechanism** — Equations, conditions, yield, selectivity
- **Analysis & structure** — Spectra, structure evidence, quantitation where relevant
- **Safety & ethics** — Reagents, waste, toxicity, lab safety where relevant
- **Citation** — Literature and databases support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before lab / mechanism reports | Academic review |
| Spectra, literature, conditions | Source check |
| Reactions & mechanisms cramming | Concise mode |
| Lab report drafts & safety | Writing review |
| chemistry slides, seminar, poster | Presentation |
| chemistry study, exams, projects | Study companion |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for Reaxys, PubChem, and SDS sheets.
- Edit equations and mechanisms with structures in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for spectra, yields, and safety data.
- Organise long lab reports in **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No hazardous synthesis, illegal substances, or safety bypass**
