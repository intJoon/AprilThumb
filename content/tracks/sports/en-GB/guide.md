# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## sports science assignment tips

- **Physiology & load** — Training load, adaptation, recovery evidence
- **Measurement & stats** — n, effect size, validity where relevant
- **Safety** — Injury, overtraining, contraindications where relevant
- **Citation** — Literature and data support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before training / physiology reports | Academic review |
| Literature, measures, n, effect size | Source check |
| Protocol & load cramming | Concise mode |
| Program & injury-prevention slides | Presentation |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for ACSM, physiology, and load-monitoring sources.
- Refine training and measurement tables in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for VO2max, biomechanics, and effect sizes.
- Organise long programme reports in **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No training or supplements ignoring injury or illness**
- Pain, cardiac, or breathing symptoms → medical/urgent care
