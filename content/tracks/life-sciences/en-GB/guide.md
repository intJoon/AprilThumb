# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## life sciences assignment tips

- **Biological mechanism** — Pathway, genetics, cell, ecology made concrete
- **Experiment & data** — Design, controls, n, stats, reproducibility where relevant
- **Limits** — Model organism, in vitro limits, generalisation scope
- **Citation** — Papers and databases support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before bio report / fieldwork | Academic review |
| Papers, data, protocol citations | Source check |
| Pathways & terms cramming | Concise mode |
| Lab / field report drafts | Writing review |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for PubMed, UniProt, and databases.
- Refine experimental design and figure captions in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for protocols, stats, and reproducibility notes.
- Paste long review drafts into **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No biosafety or gene-editing regulation bypass**
