# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## economics assignment tips

- **Model & assumptions** — Assumptions, equilibrium, comparative statics where relevant
- **Identification & data** — Endogeneity, sample, units, robustness where relevant
- **Welfare & implications** — Elasticity, externality, distribution where relevant
- **Citation** — Papers and data support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before model / essay / policy work | Academic review |
| Data, papers, statistics cited | Source check |
| Graphs, models, assumptions cramming | Concise mode |
| Analysis / policy memo drafts | Writing review |
| economics slides, seminar, poster | Presentation |
| economics study, exams, projects | Study companion |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for NBER, RePEc, and official statistics.
- Refine models and graph explanations in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for datasets and identification strategies.
- Organise long empirical reports in **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No investment, tax, or legal decisions** — concepts and coursework
