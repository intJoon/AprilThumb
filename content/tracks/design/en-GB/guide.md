# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## design assignment tips

- **Research** — Interviews, tests, personas evidenced where relevant
- **Decisions & alternatives** — Rationale, trade-offs, accessibility
- **Validation** — Prototype, metrics, limits
- **Citation** — Cases, literature, captions support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before portfolio / UX work | Academic review |
| Research, tests, case citations | Source check |
| Persona & flow cramming | Concise mode |
| UX story & process draft review | Writing review |
| Prototype & slide decks | Presentation |
| Design / UX study & projects | Study companion |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for UX cases and accessibility guidance.
- Refine wireframes and captions together in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for Nielsen, WCAG, and benchmarks.
- Organise long portfolio narratives in **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No client PII in portfolios**
