# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## business & accounting assignment tips

- **Problem definition** — Stakeholders, constraints, objectives clear
- **Analysis & evidence** — Financial, market, operations data and frameworks
- **Alternatives & trade-offs** — Options, risks, feasibility
- **Citation** — Cases, reports, accounting standards support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before case / strategy / accounting | Academic review |
| Reports, financial & market data | Source check |
| Frameworks & metrics cramming | Concise mode |
| Memo / strategy report drafts | Writing review |
| business & accounting slides, seminar, poster | Presentation |
| business & accounting study, exams, projects | Study companion |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for 10-K, IR, and accounting standard summaries.
- Organise financial and case analysis in **Canvas** tables.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for market, competitor, and IFRS/GAAP sources.
- Paste long case memos into **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No real investment, tax, or corporate decisions**
