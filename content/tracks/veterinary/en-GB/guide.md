# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## veterinary medicine assignment tips

- **Species & dose** — Species, mg/kg, route explicit
- **Pathophysiology & evidence** — Species-specific mechanism and evidence
- **Withdrawal & food animals** — MRL, withdrawal, off-label limits where relevant
- **Citation** — Veterinary sources support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before vet case / clinical report | Academic review |
| Species, dose, MRL, citations | Source check |
| Drug / pathophys by species | Concise mode |
| Case draft & treatment logic | Writing review |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for Plumb's, vet papers, and MRL sources.
- Organise species doses and cases in **Canvas** tables.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for mg/kg, withdrawal, and vet guidelines.
- Paste long clinical reports into **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No real animal diagnosis, prescription, or dosing**
- Emergencies → veterinarian or urgent care
