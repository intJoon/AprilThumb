# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## education assignment tips

- **Goals & standards** — Learning objectives, assessment, curriculum linked
- **Evidence** — Educational theory, research, policy evidence
- **Practice & reflection** — Lesson, observation, improvement, limits
- **Citation** — Literature and policy support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before lesson / design / research | Academic review |
| Pedagogy literature & theory cited | Source check |
| Theory, strategies, assessment cramming | Concise mode |
| Lesson plan / research draft | Writing review |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for curricula, rubrics, and education research.
- Organise lesson plans and objectives in **Canvas** tables.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for standards, cases, and observation checklists.
- Paste long portfolio drafts into **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No exposing student/child PII**
- Suspected abuse → reporting and professional channels
