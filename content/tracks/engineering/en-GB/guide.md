# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## engineering assignment tips

- **Numbers & units** — Consistent units, dimensions, error, uncertainty
- **Model & design** — Assumptions, boundary conditions, alternatives
- **Balance & performance** — Mass/energy, circuits, structures as relevant
- **Safety & codes** — HAZOP, margins, standards, regulations where relevant

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before design / calc / PFD work | Academic review |
| Standards, literature, numbers | Source check |
| Formulas, units, balances cramming | Concise mode |
| Design report / drawing drafts | Writing review |
| engineering slides, seminar, poster | Presentation |
| engineering study, exams, projects | Study companion |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for standards, HAZOP, and datasheets.
- Edit equations and units with notes in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for ASME, IEC, and manufacturer manuals.
- Organise long calculations and PFD notes in **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No hazardous experiments or code bypass**
- Lab/field safety follows instructor and regulations
