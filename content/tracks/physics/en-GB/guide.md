# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## physics assignment tips

- **Physical model** — Assumptions, approximations, validity range
- **Equations & units** — Dimensional consistency, derivation, significant figures
- **Experiment** — Setup, error, reproducibility, data interpretation
- **Citation** — Texts and papers support claims and numbers

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before physics coursework / lab | Academic review |
| Literature, spectra, numbers cited | Source check |
| Formulas, definitions cramming | Concise mode |
| Lab report / proof drafts | Writing review |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for texts, papers, and CODATA constants.
- Refine derivations step-by-step in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for units, errors, and experiment conditions.
- Paste long lab reports into **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No unsafe radiation/high-voltage experiments**
