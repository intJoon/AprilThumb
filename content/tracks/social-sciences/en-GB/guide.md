# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## social sciences assignment tips

- **Theory & concepts** — Operationalisation and theoretical frame explicit
- **Methods & data** — Sample, design, bias, ethics where relevant
- **Interpretation & limits** — Correlation vs causation, generalisation, alternatives
- **Citation** — Literature and data support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before survey / field / policy report | Academic review |
| Sources, methods, sample citations | Source check |
| Theory, concepts, variables cramming | Concise mode |
| Report / argument drafts | Writing review |
| social sciences slides, seminar, poster | Presentation |
| social sciences study, exams, projects | Study companion |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for surveys, policy reports, and IRB guidance.
- Refine variables and sampling notes in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for open data and coding manuals.
- Paste long qualitative drafts into **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No collecting or exposing real PII**
- Research ethics and IRB per instructor guidance
