# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## cultural arts assignment tips

- **Primary sources** — Works, programmes, archives, exhibitions evidenced
- **Context** — Period, institution, reception, policy
- **Interpretation** — Claims tied to sources; alternative readings
- **Citation** — Catalogues and papers consistent

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before critique / portfolio work | Academic review |
| Works, literature, context cited | Source check |
| Theory, context, works cramming | Concise mode |
| Critique / interpretation drafts | Writing review |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for archives, catalogues, and programmes.
- Refine interpretation and citations with sources in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for JSTOR, museum databases, and reviews.
- Paste long essay drafts into **Google Docs**.
