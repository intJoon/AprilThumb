# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## home economics assignment tips

- **Household & consumer context** — Nutrition, food, household, welfare explicit
- **Evidence & indicators** — Sample, indicators, data sources
- **Policy & limits** — RCT/survey limits, generalisation scope
- **Citation** — Data and literature support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before nutrition / policy report | Academic review |
| Guidelines, data, recipe citations | Source check |
| Nutrition, safety, protocol cramming | Concise mode |
| Practicum / policy report drafts | Writing review |
| home economics slides, seminar, poster | Presentation |
| home economics study, exams, projects | Study companion |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for nutrition databases, HACCP, and policy reports.
- Refine diet and indicator tables in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for RDA, food safety, and survey data.
- Organise long policy reports in **Google Docs**.

## Caution

- **No personalised medical nutrition prescriptions**
