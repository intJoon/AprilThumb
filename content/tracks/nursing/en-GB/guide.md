# How to use this

Tap **Copy**, then paste at the start of a **new** ChatGPT or Gemini chat. After that, send your assignment, draft, or question as usual.

Pin favourites in ChatGPT **Custom instructions** or a Gemini **Gem**.

## nursing assignment tips

- **Evidence & guidelines** — Nursing evidence and care standards linked
- **Nursing process** — Assessment→diagnosis→plan→implement→evaluate coherent
- **Safety & ethics** — Patient safety, infection, privacy, ethical dilemmas
- **Citation** — Texts and guidelines support claims

## Which prompt when?

| Situation | Prompt |
|------|----------|
| Before care plan / SOAP | Academic review |
| Nursing guidelines & citations | Source check |
| Diagnosis / intervention cramming | Concise mode |
| Care plan draft review | Writing review |

Use **Academic review** and **Study companion** in separate chats.

## In ChatGPT

- **Web search** for nursing guidelines and Cochrane summaries.
- Edit care plans and SOAP step-by-step in **Canvas**.

## In Gemini

- **Google Search** for NANDA and clinical guideline links.
- Paste long care plans into **Google Docs** for review.

## Caution

- **No real patient care or medication orders** — learning only
- Self-harm or emergency → professional help
